What is HACKATHON?

Engineering from A to Z

Hackathon 2023 is an event organized by BEST Skopje in partnership with the company IT Labs. In this exciting 24-hour competition, by solving current programming problems, you can develop your engineering skills, teamwork, critical, creative, and logical thinking. Gain invaluable practical experience working on a real problem. Also, you will get to know the top professionals at IT Labs, with whom you may collaborate in the future.

Web Development

Web development is the process of developing a website, application, or software. It involves determining user needs and creating an attractive interface, as well as building functionality. Students will solve tasks related to this field.
