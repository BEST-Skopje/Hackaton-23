Често Поставувани Прашања

  • What is the Hackathon?

    A hackathon is a 12-hour competition in the field of programming, where participants with similar interests come together in teams to provide a solution to a given problem.

  • Who can apply?

    Any regular student from the first or second cycle of studies at the University "St. Cyril and Methodius" - Skopje can apply.

  • How many members should each team have?

    Each team should consist of 3 to 5 participants.

  • Can I apply if I don't have a team?

    Yes, participants who are not registered in a team will be assigned a team.

  • Where can I apply?

    You can apply through the registration form at this link .

  • Does every team member need to apply?

    Yes, each team member needs to apply individually, providing the team name and the names of the other team members in the form.

  • When and where will the BEST Hackathon take place?

    It will take place on 24.12. at the premises of ITLabs .

  • What kind of problem will be solved?

    The problem will be in the field of web development. Once selected as participants, you will receive additional information.

  • Will equipment be provided, or do we need to bring our own?

    Participants need to bring their own necessary equipment for work.

  • Which programming language should be used?

    You can use any programming languages and technologies familiar to you in the field of web development.

  • Do I need to be a student of a technical faculty?

    In each team of 3, there must be a minimum of 1 participant studying computer science or having knowledge in computer science, and in each team of 5, a minimum of 2 participants with such background.
